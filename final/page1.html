Firthview Farm Café

Located in the rolling hills of Firthview, just a short drive from Edinburgh, Scotland. Nestled on the scenic Firthview Farm, this charming café offers visitors a warm, rustic atmosphere paired with stunning views of the Firth of Forth. Established in 2015, the Firthview Farm Café prides itself on its farm-to-table philosophy, serving freshly prepared meals made with ingredients sourced directly from the farm and local suppliers. Whether you're stopping in for a cup of freshly brewed coffee or a hearty homemade lunch, the café has something to satisfy every palate.

Menu Highlights:

  • Firthview Full Scottish Breakfast
    Start your day with the ultimate Scottish breakfast. This hearty dish features:
    • Local sausages and bacon from nearby farms
    • Fresh eggs from the farm’s own hens
    • Tattie scones, a beloved Scottish staple
    • Grilled tomatoes, perfectly roasted
    • Traditional black pudding, rich and flavorful
    A classic breakfast that will fuel you for a day of exploration around Firthview and beyond.
  • Farmhouse Soup of the Day
    Our rotating selection of seasonal soups is made with the freshest ingredients, sourced directly from the farm. The soups vary by season, but some favorites include:
    • Leek and Potato Soup made with tender leeks and creamy potatoes from the farm
    • Tomato and Basil Soup, featuring ripe tomatoes harvested at peak flavor
    • Butternut Squash Soup in the autumn, with the sweetness of squash and hints of nutmeg
    Served with a slice of freshly baked bread, it’s a comforting option any time of year.
  • Homemade Scones and Cakes
    No visit to the café is complete without trying our homemade baked goods, straight from the oven. Choose from:
    • Traditional Scottish scones served warm with thick clotted cream and fruity jam
    • Raspberry Shortbread made with farm-fresh berries and a buttery crumb
    • Chocolate and Orange Cake or Carrot Cake (depending on the season), perfect with a hot drink for an afternoon treat
  • Firthview Farm Burger
    Our signature burger is a farm-to-table classic, featuring:
    • Grass-fed beef from the farm’s own cattle, hand-ground to create the juiciest patty
    • Locally sourced cheese, melted to perfection
    • Crisp fresh greens picked from the farm’s garden
    • Tangy homemade chutney, adding the perfect zing
    Served in a freshly baked bun with hand-cut fries, it’s a satisfying meal that captures the essence of Scotland's finest produce.
