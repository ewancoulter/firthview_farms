hello

Welcome to Firthview Farm, a charming and welcoming farm nestled between the historic cities of Edinburgh and Carlisle. Originally a working farm, Firthview Farm has been thoughtfully transformed into a unique visitor attraction that offers a delightful blend of rural life and family-friendly experiences. Whether you're visiting to explore the picturesque countryside, enjoy hands-on activities at the petting zoo, or savor delicious food at the farm's café, Firthview Farm promises a memorable day out for all ages. Come and experience the heartwarming atmosphere of this hidden gem in Scotland's countryside!

About Firthview Farm

Firthview Farm is a peaceful and scenic working farm nestled in the heart of the beautiful Scottish countryside, conveniently located between Edinburgh and Carlisle. Originally established as a small family-run farm, it has been lovingly transformed into a welcoming visitor attraction, offering a perfect day out for families, nature lovers, and anyone looking to experience authentic farm life.
