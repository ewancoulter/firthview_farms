Welcome to Firthview Farms Petting Zoo!

Nestled in the rolling hills of the countryside, Firthview Farms is home to a charming petting zoo where families can get up close and personal with a variety of friendly farm animals. The zoo is designed to give visitors a hands-on experience with animals, creating a fun, educational atmosphere for all ages.

Animals You’ll Meet:

  • Friendly goats: These curious creatures love to be fed and petted, and are always eager to interact with visitors. Watch out for their playful antics!
  • Cuddly bunnies: Soft and gentle, the rabbits are perfect for a cozy cuddle, and the kids love feeding them their favorite veggies.
  • Fainting goats: A unique and hilarious breed, these goats ‘faint’ when startled, collapsing briefly before jumping up again. They’re a crowd favorite!
  • Miniature ponies: These pint-sized ponies are perfect for a gentle ride around the petting zoo and will make you feel like you’ve stepped into a storybook.
  • Pigs and piglets: The farm has a happy group of pigs who love to root around in the mud, along with adorable piglets that are always looking for a snack.
  • Llamas and alpacas: Known for their fluffy coats and gentle demeanor, these animals are always a hit with visitors. Don’t forget to snap a photo!
  • Chickens and ducks: Kids can help collect eggs and watch the playful ducks waddling around their ponds.

education

The petting zoo is not just about interacting with the animals—it's also an educational experience. Each animal is part of the larger ecosystem, and we encourage visitors to learn more about how they live, what they eat, and how they’re cared for. It's a perfect opportunity for children to develop a love for animals and nature, while families make lasting memories together.
